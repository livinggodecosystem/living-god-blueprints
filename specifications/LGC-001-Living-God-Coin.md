## Overview

Living God Coin (LGC) is the native utility and governance token of Living God Ecosystem.

The token is designed to provide a secure, transparent, and fixed-supply economic foundation for the ecosystem while supporting entrepreneurship, governance, ecosystem participation, and future infrastructure.

The token contract intentionally minimizes complexity in Version 1 to maximize security, auditability, and long-term maintainability.

## Objectives

The Version 1 token contract shall:

✓ Implement the ERC-20 standard.

✓ Maintain a permanently fixed maximum supply.

✓ Support secure transfers.

✓ Support approvals and allowances.

✓ Integrate cleanly with Treasury and Vesting contracts.

✓ Follow OpenZeppelin security standards.

The Version 1 token contract shall NOT include staking, DeFi, governance logic, buyback mechanisms, or transaction taxes.
